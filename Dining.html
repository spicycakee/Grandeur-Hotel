Grandeur's CRU Steakhouse

Complement your meal with an array of delectable sides, from buttery mashed potatoes to crisp, seasonal vegetables. Open for dinner, our Manila Steakhouse restaurant is the perfect respite after a day of business, shopping, or sight-seeing. Our menu is a symphony of flavors, featuring a curated selection of prime, hand-selected cuts of meat, each aged to perfection. Whether you prefer a tender filet mignon, a marbled ribeye, or a hearty T-bone, our skilled chefs expertly grill your steak to your desired level of doneness.

Pair your steak with a glass of fine wine from our extensive cellar, curated to enhance the flavors of your meal, or explore our craft cocktail menu for a unique and refreshing experience.

Grandeur International Restaurant

Savor distinctive local cuisine, tasty international favorites and expansive buffets when you dine at Grandeur International Restaurant. This inviting, all-day Manila buffet restaurant is found just steps from your hotel room. Our menu is a treasure trove of international delights, offering an array of dishes inspired by the culinary traditions of various countries. Indulge in the fragrant spices of Indian curries, savor the artful presentation of Japanese sushi, or dive into the comforting embrace of Italian pasta.
