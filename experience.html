Experiences

Enjoy leisure time at
Grandeur Hotel



Whether you are traveling for business or leisure, it's important to make the most of your experience in a new city. But that comes at a price - exploring can be tiring so we always recommend balancing new experiences with some leisure time. At Grandeur Hotel we offer a range of leisure activities ranging from a well-equipped fitness centre to an on-site spa and adjoining golf course. So no matter how you enjoy winding down, you'll find the perfect way to do it at Grandeur Hotel.

Quan Spa

Indulge in a journey of relaxation as our expert therapists guide you through a personalized spa experience. The soothing ambiance, infused with soft lighting and calming aromas, sets the stage for a sensory escape from the demands of the outside world. For those seeking a more immersive escape, Quan Spa offers specialized wellness packages, combining a variety of treatments for a comprehensive and deeply rejuvenating experience. Whether you're looking for a quick revitalization or a full day of pampering, our spa packages cater to every desire. Immerse yourself in the art of relaxation at Quan Spa, where each visit is a journey towards a renewed and revitalized you.

> 9:00 AM - 9:00 PM

Gym

Embark on a transformative fitness journey at our state-of-the-art gym, where health meets luxury in a space meticulously crafted to elevate your well-being. Our hotel's fitness center is more than just a workout facility; it's a haven for wellness enthusiasts and fitness aficionados alike. Our certified fitness trainers are on hand to provide personalized guidance, crafting workout routines tailored to your individual needs and aspirations. From high-intensity interval training to mindful yoga sessions, our diverse fitness classes cater to a variety of preferences, making your journey towards a healthier lifestyle both enjoyable and effective. At our hotel, we understand that wellness is a holistic experience.

Dive into a world of aquatic bliss at our exquisite hotel, where the enchanting allure of swimming awaits you.
